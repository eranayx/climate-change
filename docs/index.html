Why Climate Change Matters

“Studies based on IPCC data say there’s a 95% chance we’ll pass 2°C by the year 2100 and the detrimental effects of climate change may be unavoidable” — Popular Science, YouTube.

Climate change is known as long-term changes to temperature and weather patterns. This presents a deadly problem for virtually everything — from humans, to animals, and even plants. These shifts, natural or caused by humans, create abnormal adjustments to the weather that prove more disadvantageous than beneficial.
